Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of massage do you offer?

We offer a range of therapeutic massage services including:

  • - **Swedish Massage** – A relaxing, full-body massage that focuses on soothing muscles and improving circulation.
  • - **Deep Tissue Massage** – Targets deeper layers of muscle to relieve chronic tension and pain.
  • - **Sports Massage** – Designed for athletes or individuals with active lifestyles, it helps prevent injuries and improve flexibility.
  • - **Aromatherapy Massage** – Combines massage with essential oils to enhance relaxation and well-being.
  • - **Hot Stone Massage** – Uses heated stones to relax muscles and improve circulation.
  • - **Pregnancy Massage** – A gentle, safe massage designed to ease discomfort during pregnancy.

2. How should I prepare for my massage?

  • - Arrive a few minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.
  • - Wear comfortable clothing, but be prepared to undress to your level of comfort during the massage.
  • - If you have any specific areas of tension or pain, let your therapist know beforehand so they can focus on those areas.
  • - Avoid heavy meals or alcohol before your session to ensure you’re relaxed and comfortable.

Is massage safe during pregnancy?**

Yes, we offer pregnancy massage specifically tailored to the needs of expectant mothers. Our therapists are trained in techniques that ensure the safety and comfort of both the mother and baby. Always consult your doctor before booking if you have any concerns.

#### **4. How long does a massage session last?**

Sessions can range from 30 minutes to 90 minutes, depending on your preferences and the type of massage you choose. We recommend starting with at least 60 minutes for a thorough session.

#### **5. What should I expect during my first visit?**

During your first visit, your therapist will ask you a few questions about your health history, any areas of pain or tension, and your goals for the massage. This helps tailor the session to your needs. You will then enjoy a relaxing and therapeutic massage experience.

#### **6. Do you offer gift cards?**

Yes! We offer gift cards for all of our services. They make the perfect gift for birthdays, holidays, or just to show someone you care.

#### **7. How do I book an appointment?**

You can book your appointment online through our website or by calling us directly. We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekend or evening appointments, as they tend to fill up quickly.

#### **8. What is your cancellation policy?**

We require at least 24 hours' notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. If you cancel within 24 hours, you may be charged a cancellation fee.

#### **9. What should I wear during the massage?**

You can undress to your comfort level. The therapist will leave the room while you get ready, and you will be covered with a sheet during the massage, with only the areas being worked on exposed.

#### **10. Can massage help with chronic pain or stress?**

Yes, massage therapy is known to help reduce stress, alleviate muscle tension, improve circulation, and provide relief from chronic pain. Your therapist will adjust the techniques based on your individual needs to address any discomfort or pain you may have.

#### **11. Do you offer couples massages?**

Yes, we offer couples massage sessions where two people can enjoy side-by-side treatments in the same room. It’s a great way to relax together!

#### **12. Are the massage therapists licensed?**

Yes, all of our massage therapists are licensed and certified in their respective fields. They have undergone extensive training and continuing education to provide the best care possible.
